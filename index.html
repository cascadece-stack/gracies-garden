About Us

We cultivate Golden Oyster, Lion’s Mane, and Pink, Blue & Italian Oyster mushrooms. Our mushrooms are prized for their texture, flavour, and nutritional benefits. Add them to pastas, sautés, or enjoy as meat alternatives!

Lion’s Mane is a unique mushroom known for its shaggy appearance and delicious taste. With a mild, seafood-like flavor and potential brain-boosting benefits, it's a favorite among gourmet and health enthusiasts. Stay tuned for a detailed write-up coming soon.
