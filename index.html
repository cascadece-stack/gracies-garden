Welcome to Gracie's Garden

At Gracie's Garden, we cultivate delicious and nutritious mushrooms right here in Gulf Breeze, Florida. Our urban farm focuses on sustainable growing practices and provides fresh gourmet mushrooms to our community. Whether you’re a chef looking for locally grown produce or a home cook eager to try something new, our mushrooms bring a unique flavor to every dish.

Explore our site to learn more about our story, our growing practices, and how you can purchase our mushrooms.

Freshly harvested Golden Oyster mushrooms

We harvest golden oyster mushrooms daily, offering 8 oz portions for $7. Their delicate texture and mild flavour make them perfect for stir‑fries, soups and more.

Discover Lion’s Mane mushrooms

Lion’s Mane mushrooms are prized for their meaty texture and subtle seafood flavour. Add them to pasta dishes, sauté them as a steak alternative or brew your own adaptogenic tea.

Experience our Pink, Blue & Italian Oyster mushrooms

In addition to golden oysters, we grow vibrant pink and blue oyster mushrooms and robust Italian oysters—each with unique colors, textures and flavors.
